Our Project

We're an independent, student-led project aiming to assist Form 5 Students who lack access to affordable tuition with SPM Preparation for free.

We aim to help Form 5 students who are currently struggling with their academics due to the disruption of classes and lack of access to affordable tuition classes during this challenging time. We realised that not everyone has the chance to obtain one-to-one guidance from teachers on topics they may struggle with. Therefore, we are here to help!
